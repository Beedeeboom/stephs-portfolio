My Values & Passion

Growing up I shared my father’s passion for arts and all things creative and later followed in his tracks and became a qualified graphic designer myself. For me Graphic Design is not just putting together a visual solution for my clients but bringing meaning to every design I create. I've recently found a new passion in coding and here I am on my way to becoming a developer!

Reliability

I know my clients or potential employers don’t just want to hire a developer with talent, they want to hire someone they can count on. I believe it’s important to develop consistency and to work with a team or client to meet their deadlines.

Openess

I believe constructive criticism helps us grow to become better at what we do. I know it takes time to create the perfect design or develop the right code and being open to feedback from your team or client is essential. My aim is for my clients/employer to be 100% happy with my work

Passion

I love what I do and my passion for web development and design is what drives me to give my absolute best when working on every project. I love to bring meaning to every piece of code or design that I create.
